Excel Interiors specializes in re-upholstery, repair and refinishing of furniture. I gave them a complete re-design of their existing website to offer a more clean and modern feel using:
- Wix
- 123 Form Builder

The homepage features a full-width carousel which displays popular navigational options for ease of use.

The service pages feature left aligned high resolution images coupled with content that describes each service and a list of work that's offered within that particular service.

The blog page displays the most recent blog post at the top with a convenient right sidebar for users to browse and read through older posts.

I included a blog for SEO purposes, because the websites content is made to be clean, simple and to the point, so it doesn't confuse users.

The gallery page features a full-width carousel to keep the aesthetic features of the homepage.

The contact page clearly displays Excel Interiors' contact information and their hours of operation.

An important feature Excel Interiors wanted was an image uploading option to allow customers to send pictures of their furniture for estimates, so I included exactly that.

At the bottom, I embedded Google Maps for users to easily locate the business relative to where they live.
